About Us

We expertise in providing end to end solution to the clients for even the most demanding logistics requirements to or from almost any point on the globe. Services

Services

Air Freight & Sea Freight Consolidations

SpeedoFreight has the expertise, facilities and services to meet the needs of all air freight users, both importers and exporters. Our experienced operations specialists offer efficient and professional services with the assurance of prompt delivery of cargo to major destinations throughout the world.

Break Bulk Cargo

The Bulk Handling division of SpeedoFreight, specializing in bulk and break bulk cargo operations, is an efficient and professionally managed maritime logistics service provider which provides comprehensive transport and logistics services across all major ports

Customs Clearance

SpeedoFreight operations personnels will assist our valued clients to determine the tariff classification, developing indirect tax and customs duty, identifying and minimising the impact of non tariff barriers to trade and effect transfer pricing mechanisms.

Freight Booking & Chartering

We'll assist in determining the best charter solution for your air freight shipment needs and research carrier availability and negotiate the best charter price for your budget, for ultra-critical shipments air charter is the premier choice. Our air charter pricing is customized specifically to your actual needs combined with determining the ideal aircraft type and position to ensure that your urgent air cargo arrives on time.

International Courier Services

With SpeedoFreight, Shipping around the world is as easy as shipping domestically. Whether it's business documents or packages, you can be rest assured that your shipments will have the most reliable service available and fastest transit time to virtually any destination within India or globally.

Warehousing

The warehousing service generally encompasses the storage of goods for future or staggered delivery according to our client’s schedules and requirements. This facilities can be extended into a bonded warehousing with stock and inventory control all bundled in a package.

Transportations

SpeedoFreight always stand ready to deliver full or loose container load shipments anywhere with competitive rates and ensure all orders are delivered accurately and arrive to destination on time. SpeedoFreight modern, reliable and well equipped truck, ensures fast, safe and efficient delivery service.

Specialized & Other Value added services.

We carry Pharmaceutical products, Chemicals, Hazardous goods, Perishable cargo and personal effect shipments to most other destinations.
